Construction
  • 24×24 grid, cells punched at 7×7 with rx 2
  • Tile radius scales at 24% of the mark — soft, not pill
  • One cell knocked out bottom-left to the surface below; a faint second sits top-right at 32%
  • Tile is a single solid --teal — the cutouts carry the contrast, no extra fill
  • On an accent surface, flip it: a --surface-2 tile with --teal cells

Guidance
Do
Keep one strong cutout plus a faint second. Let the tile stay a single solid colour. On accent surfaces, invert to a surface tile with teal cells.
Don't
Don't punch more than two cutouts, gradient-fill the tile, outline the cells, or drop the teal tile onto a busy or photographic background.
